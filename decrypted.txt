Companies in the information technology sector are active as manufacturers or distributors of hardware and software. The business sectors of software, data services, IT services, semiconductors, technologically sophisticated hardware, mobile phones, computers and accessories are included. Manufacturers of computer games, on the other hand, are assigned to the communications services sector. Thematic ETFs are available for several sub-sectors of the information technology sector. For example, cloud technology ETFs, cybersecurity ETFs or digitalisation ETFs track specific topics from the information technology sector.
